About me

I'm Leo, a Computer Science and Software Engineering student at the University Of Washington Bothell. I started my coding journey by learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which later led me to explore more advanced languages like C++ and Python. My goal is to become a full stack developer with a focus on database management and engineering.

I aim to use my creative drive and logical approach to problem solving in order to become a developer who writes meaningful code-solutions that not only function well but have a positive impact on society.

Even from a young age, I've been fascinated by how things are made. I would often look around and wonder how things were built or even how they could be improved. Today, I apply this same sense of curiosity and attention to detail to the porjects I build, always thinking about how functionality, design, and user experience can be refined.
